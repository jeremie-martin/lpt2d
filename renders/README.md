# Renders

This directory is for saved render artifacts and short notes about how to keep
them organized.

## What Belongs Here

- curated preview or HQ renders worth keeping
- contact sheets or representative stills
- outputs kept for discussion, validation, or comparison

## What Does Not

- source scene definitions
- the canonical example pack
- bulk generated output with no retention purpose

Scenes and authored intent belong in [`scenes/`](/home/holo/prog/lpt2d/scenes)
and [`examples/`](/home/holo/prog/lpt2d/examples), not in render artifacts.
